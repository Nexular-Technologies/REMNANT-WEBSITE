    Financial Planning

    Remnant believes that a financial plan acts as a guide as you go through life’s journey. It helps you stay in control of your income, expenses, and investments to manage your money and achieve your goals.

    We service individuals, families, and businesses who need objective assistance in organising their financial affairs. We are passionate about providing sound advice to those who are serious about improving their financial situations.

    Our Professional Approach

    We utilize the FPI-approved, 6-step financial planning process. Remnant advisors provide independent financial advice and are wholeheartedly committed to acting in the best interests of their clients at all times.

    Estate Planning

    Estate planning deals with the assessment and management of assets to ensure the estate is distributed according to the owner's wishes. It protects your beneficiaries from legal hassles in the event of your passing.

    The Role of Trusts: A trust allows the trustee the right to hold title to property for the benefit of the beneficiary. This can protect assets from creditors and potentially reduce estate duties.

    Comprehensive Planning:
    • Will drafting
    • Trust management
    • Succession planning
    • Asset restructuring
    • Business assurance
    • Tax considerations
    • Offshore structures
    Retirement Planning

    Retirement planning is the process of setting income goals and identifying the actions necessary to achieve them. Majority of people underprovide for retirement; Remnant strives to change that picture.

    A solid plan gives the client and advisor a clearer picture of the financial position. Every decision that you make today contributes towards your future self at retirement.

    "Every decision you make today contributes towards your future self."

    Fiduciary Services

    A fiduciary acts independently according to the terms of the trust or estate. Having a fiduciary you can trust to deal with legal matters when you have passed away is invaluable.
