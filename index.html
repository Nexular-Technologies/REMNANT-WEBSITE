    Financial Planning

    Remnant approaches financial planning using the FPI approved, 6 step financial planning process. We believe a financial plan acts as a guide through life's journey, staying in control of income, expenses, and investments to achieve your lifestyle objectives.

    Estate Planning

    Estate planning deals with the assessment and management of assets to ensure they are distributed according to your wishes. Proper planning protects beneficiaries and maximizes the benefit they receive.

    Retirement Planning

    Retirement planning is the process of setting income goals and the actions needed to achieve them. Every decision made today contributes to your future self and ensures financial comfort after your working years.

    Local & Offshore Investment

    Investment Planning aligns financial goals with resources to ensure surplus funds meet future objectives. As independent advisors, we offer unbiased advice across various local and offshore platforms.

    Corporate & Personal Tax Planning

    Our strategies focus on minimizing your tax burden while maximizing your investment returns. We integrate technical tax planning with an understanding of how beliefs and emotions impact financial decisions.

    Employee Benefits

    Remnant provides structures for employee benefits, enabling staff to better manage financial uncertainties. These benefits are structured to suit various lifestyles and future goals.

    Fiduciary Services

    A fiduciary acts independently according to the terms of a trust deed or estate, with your best interests in mind. Remnant uses strategic alliances to provide world-class services like Will drafting and safe custody.

    Business Risk Analysis

    Risk analysis is the process of identifying, analyzing, and prioritizing factors that may affect you financially. The goal is to minimize or prevent financial loss through clear strategies.

    Short-Term Insurance

    Short-term insurance protects you from the financial burdens of property loss. We help update your cover as the conditions of your possessions change to protect your future income.

    Healthcare Solutions

    We provide tailored medical aid and healthcare planning for individuals and families. Our solutions help bridge financial gaps for hospital-related expenses through gap cover and medical aid planning.
